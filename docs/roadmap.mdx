## How to read this page

This is the **dependency graph** of the curriculum. If you're in a hurry or
jumping in at a specific skill, you can skip forward — but every later module
assumes the concepts from earlier phases.

<Callout kind="tip" title="Rule of thumb">
The first time through, follow the order. Come back to this page when you
need a big-picture refresher, or when deciding what to revisit before an
interview.
</Callout>
